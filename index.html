HOW IT WORKS

Empty Jar.

show details

Empty jar with all ingredients into a medium sized bowl. Pre-heat oven to 370°F.

Add Water.

show details

Add 1 cup of water. Knead until you get a smooth dough. Cover bowl with clean kitchen towel. Let dough rest for 30-60 mins at a warm place. The longer the rest, the more it rises! For an even better result, knead dough a second time after 10 mins of resting.

Bake.

show details

Take dough out of bowl. Cut a slash (0.5inch) down the length of the dough. Spread water on entire top. Bake at 370°F for 45 mins. To allow a better rise, put a cup of water in oven while baking.
